Design Notes

Warm Cream Heart

Heart uses #FFF5EC instead of cold white. Softer, more personal, matches the app's emotional warmth.

Mood Pulse Wave

Bold wave line at 80% opacity and 3.5dp width with wider amplitude. Clearly visible even at launcher icon size.

Reading Dot

Center dot on the wave suggests a data point. Subtle at small sizes, characterful at large sizes.

Richer Gradient

Deeper color stops with visible radial glow. Background has perceivable depth, not invisible decorations.
